My Research Question

What are solutions to racism found in many facial recognition algorithms?

My Current Project Idea

My partner and I are working on our own facial recognition program that works on individual people by training AI to recognize their specific facial characteristics

Key Findings p1

  1. False facial recognition scans tends to disproportionately target/harm people of color and leads to wrongful arrests
  2. Facial recognition used in job hiring often favors lighter skin tones and male features which means that minority women are less likely to be hired at a company that uses biometrics

For more, check out Initial Research

Key Findings p2

  1. Many problems in biometrics stem from the training algorithms and datasets, that retain the racial bias from the programmer or statistics
  2. Facial recognition algorithms are only able to process data and unable to distinguish causation from correlation, leading them to associate peoples with false attributes

For more, check out Root Causes

Key findings p3

  1. A combination of human error and algorithmic error will often lead to people of color being harmed by racial profiling
  2. Of course the root cause of algorithmic bias is ultimately caused by humans as programmers, society, and our data that we create
